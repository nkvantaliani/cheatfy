01

Introduction

Cheatify ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application handles your information.

By using Cheatify, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

02

Information We Collect

Cheatify does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

We never collect
  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

The only data generated by the app is the cheat sheets you create, which are stored exclusively on your device.

03

AI Generation & Third Party

When you generate a cheat sheet, the topic text you enter is sent to Groq AI (api.groq.com) to generate content.

What gets sent
  • Only your topic query (e.g. "Kotlin")
  • No personal information is included
  • We do not store these queries on any server
  • Groq AI processes the request and returns generated content only

Please review Groq's Privacy Policy at groq.com/privacy.

04

Local Data Storage

All cheat sheets you generate and save are stored locally on your device using a local database (Room/SQLite).

  • We have zero access to your saved content
  • Your data never leaves your device (except topic queries to Groq AI)
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data
05

Internet Permission

Requires internet
  • Send topic queries to Groq AI API
  • Receive generated cheat sheet content
Works offline
  • View saved cheat sheets
  • Search your cheat sheets
  • Manage favorites
  • Delete cheat sheets
06

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual cheat sheets anytime from the detail screen
  • Delete all cheat sheets at once via Settings → Clear All Data
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data permanently

We do not maintain any server-side copies of your data to delete upon request.

07
