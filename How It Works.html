01 — The Basics

What is Cheatify?

Cheatify is an AI-powered Android app that generates clean, structured cheat sheets on any topic you want to learn or reference.

Whether you're a developer, student, or professional — just type a topic and get a beautifully organized cheat sheet instantly. No searching. No scrolling. Just the essentials, structured and saved.

02 — Step by Step

Generating a Cheat Sheet

01
Type Your Topic

Open the Generate tab and type any topic you want a cheat sheet for. Any topic works — technical or non-technical.

Examples
  • Kotlin Coroutines
  • SQL Joins
  • Git Commands
  • Photosynthesis
  • French Grammar Basics
  • Machine Learning Terms
02
AI Generates Content

Cheatify sends your topic to Groq AI — one of the fastest AI inference engines available. In seconds it generates a fully structured cheat sheet.

What's generated
  • Clear section titles
  • Explanations and definitions
  • Code examples (for technical topics)
  • Tables and schemas
  • Important notes and tips
  • Warnings and common errors
03
Saved Automatically

Your cheat sheet is saved instantly to your device. No manual saving needed. It appears in your Home screen immediately.

04
Access Anytime Offline

All saved cheat sheets live on your device. No internet needed to view them. Open, read, and reference them anytime — even on a plane.

04 — Offline First

Offline First Design

Cheatify is built with an offline-first approach. Your data never depends on a server — everything is stored locally using a local database.

Internet available
  • Generate new cheat sheets via Groq AI
  • Fresh content saved to your device
No internet
  • Browse all saved cheat sheets
  • Search your library
  • Manage favorites
  • Everything works offline
05 — Organization

Stay Organized

Cheatify gives you tools to manage your growing cheat sheet library.

Favorites
Star any cheat sheet to pin it to your Favorites tab for instant access.
Search
Search across all your cheat sheets by title or topic instantly.
Badges
Each cheat sheet is auto-tagged so you can filter by topic quickly.
Delete
Remove any cheat sheet from the detail screen or clear all via Settings.
06 — AI & Privacy

How AI Works in Cheatify

Cheatify uses Groq AI to generate cheat sheet content. Here is exactly what happens when you generate a cheat sheet:

Generation flow
1
You type a topic (e.g. "Kotlin Coroutines")
2
Cheatify sends only that topic text to Groq AI servers
3
Groq AI returns structured cheat sheet content
4
Content is saved to your device
5
Nothing else is sent or stored
We do NOT send
  • Your name or personal details
  • Your device information
  • Your location
  • Any previously saved cheat sheets
  • Any usage data or analytics

Your privacy is our priority. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.

07 — FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Cheatify work without internet?
Yes! All saved cheat sheets are fully accessible offline. You only need internet to generate new ones.
Is my data stored on a server?
No. All cheat sheets are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your content.
What topics can I generate cheat sheets for?
Any topic! Programming languages, frameworks, academic subjects, languages, tools, concepts — anything you can think of.
How accurate is the AI content?
Groq AI powered by Llama generates high quality content. However, always verify critical information from official documentation or sources.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. Delete individual cheat sheets from the detail screen, or delete everything at once via Settings → Clear All Data.
Is Cheatify free?
Yes, Cheatify is completely free to use.
What AI powers Cheatify?
Cheatify uses Groq AI with the Llama 3.1 model — one of the fastest and most capable open source AI models available today.
